## Approval Workflow

1. **Submission**: Submit the completed exception request to the governance body.
2. **Review**: The governance body reviews the request and may request additional information.
3. **Decision**: The governance body approves, denies, or requests modifications to the exception request.
4. **Documentation**: Approved exceptions are documented and tracked.
